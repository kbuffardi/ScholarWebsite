Programming Robots

Resources
  • Microbit Makecode - Integrated Development Environment (IDE)
  • Microbit Maqueen educational robot documentation

Part I

Explore
  • How to move forward, backward, and turn
  • How to display a message or symbol on the LED matrix
  • How to play a sound
  • How to repeat doing the above actions a specific number of times
Challenge 1

Create an algorithm that tells your robot how to celebrate, incorporating at least the following:

  • Wheel movement
  • LED Matrix display
  • Audio sounds
  • Repeat some (but not all) of these actions a specific number of times (not indefinitely)
  • Entire celebration should take between 3-10 seconds

Part II

Explore
  • What information does the light sensor provide?
  • What does it indicate on light surfaces? Dark surfaces?
Challenge 2

Program your robot to do the following:

  • When it turns on, begin driving forward
  • Continue driving forward until it reaches a surface of the opposite color (light/dark)
  • Once it has reached the opposite surface, stop moving forward
  • Begin your robot's celebration (from Challenge 1)!

Part III

Explore
  • What information does the ultrasonic sensor provide?
  • If the robot is driving toward an obstacle, what is a good distance to detect to stop the robot before colliding?
Challenge 3

Program your robot to do the following:

  • When it turns on, begin driving forward
  • Continue driving forward until it comes close to the obstacle ahead of it, but stops before colliding.
